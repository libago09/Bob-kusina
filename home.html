Welcome to Sari Filipino Kusina

Welcome to our Filipino Dish Recipe website, where we share our love for authentic Filipino cuisine with the world.
Our website features a collection of mouth-watering recipes that will take you on a culinary journey through the Philippines,
from classic adobo to hearty sinigang, to the ever-popular pancit.
Our recipes are not only delicious but also easy to follow, so you can recreate your favorite Filipino dishes in the comfort
of your own kitchen. Join us in celebrating the rich and diverse flavors of the Philippines, one recipe at a time.

Pork Sinigang

This recipe brought me back to my childhood. The sourness of the tamarind gives a burst of flavor to the pork and vegetables, making it a perfect comfort food on a cold day. It's a great way to introduce Filipino cuisine to friends who have never tried it before.
